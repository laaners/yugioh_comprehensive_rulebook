Example Question:
My "World Gears of Theurlogical Demiurgy" was Link Summoned using "Knightmare Incarnation Idlee", "World Legacy Guardragon Mardark", and "Deus X-Krawler" as materials. If that "World Gears of Theurlogical Demiurgy" is banished by the effect of "Interdimensional Matter Transporter" until the End Phase, which of its effects will be applied after it returns to the field?
Answer:
If a "World Gears of Theurlogical Demiurgy" that was Link Summoned using 3 monsters with different Types and Attributes as materials is banished by the effect of "Interdimensional Matter Transporter", its effect that makes it unaffected by other monsters' effects applies again after it returns to the field. Afterward, during your Main Phase, you will not be able to activate its effect that destroys all other cards on the field.

Ritual, Fusion, Synchro, Xyz, Link Materials 		✔️ ✔️[2][16]
Example Question:
A "Neo Blue-Eyes Ultimate Dragon" that was Fusion Summoned using 3 copies of "Blue-Eyes White Dragon" as materials is banished by the effect of "Interdimensional Matter Transporter" until the End Phase, then returned to the field.
Afterward, if I activate "De-Fusion" targeting that "Neo Blue-Eyes Ultimate Dragon", can I Special Summon its materials from the Graveyard?
Answer:
In this scenario, you can apply the part of the effect of "De-Fusion" that Special Summons all the materials used for the returned Fusion Monster's Fusion Summon, so you can Special Summon the 3 "Blue-Eyes White Dragon"s from your Graveyard.
Which specific method or effect performed that monster's Summon
Monsters being Summoned by their own specific method 		❌[23]
Example Question:
By its own effect, "The Tripper Mercury" is Tribute Summoned by Tributing 3 monsters. Then, if "Book of Moon" is activated, and "The Tripper Mercury" is changed to face-down Defense Position, what happens to the effect that makes my opponent's monsters lose ATK equal to their original ATK?
Answer:
If "The Tripper Mercury" is Tribute Summoned by Tributing 3 monsters, and is then changed to face-down Defense Position by the effect of "Book of Moon", the effect that reduces the ATK of your opponent's monsters stops applying. (Afterward, even if "The Tripper Mercury" is flipped face-up, this effect does not reapply.)
✔️[22]
Example Question:
"The Tripper Mercury" was Tribute Summoned by Tributing 3 monsters. If the effect of "Farfa, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss" is activated, and "The Tripper Mercury" is banished until the End Phase, will its effect that lowers the ATK of my opponent's monsters still apply afterward?
Answer:
If "The Tripper Mercury" was Tribute Summoned by Tributing 3 monsters, and then afterward banished by the effect of "Farfa, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss", the effect that lowers the ATK of your opponent's monsters stops applying while "The Tripper Mercury" is banished. Then, when "The Tripper Mercury" returns to the field, the effect that lowers the ATK of your opponent's monsters will apply again.
Where that monster was Summoned from ❌[15][20]
Example Question:
There is a face-down Defense Position monster on my opponent's field, which was at one point Special Summoned from the Graveyard.
In this situation, can I activate "Exosister Arment" during my turn, which is only possible if my opponent controls a monster which was Special Summoned from the Graveyard?
Answer:
If a monster is Special Summoned from the Graveyard, that monster is treated as a monster that was Special Summoned from the Graveyard, regardless of its battle position. This includes a monster which was Special Summoned from the Graveyard in face-down Defense Position, and then flipped face-up.
If any such monster is on your opponent's field, you can activate "Exosister Arment" during your turn. (As that monster is treated as having been Special Summoned from the Graveyard.)
However, if a face-up monster on the field which was Special Summoned from the Graveyard is flipped face-down, that monster is no longer treated as a monster that was Special Summoned from the Graveyard.
✔️[19]
Example Question:
A monster on my opponent's field, which was Special Summoned from the Graveyard, is temporarily banished by "Interdimensional Matter Transporter", then returns to the field.
In this situation, can I activate "Exosister Arment" during my turn, which is only possible if my opponent controls a monster which was Special Summoned from the Graveyard?
Answer:
Yes, you can.
(If a monster is Special Summoned from the Graveyard, and then banished temporarily, it is still treated as a monster that was Special Summoned from the Graveyard after returning to the field.)

If "Pahunder" is flipped face-down by "Book of Moon" during a turn in which its effect has been activated and resolved, but then is flipped face-up again by "Book of Taiyou" later that turn, can "Pahunder"'s effect be activated again?

If that monster's Battle Position has been changed this turn (if retains it can't change Battle Position again) ✔️[3] ❌
If that monster has declared an attack this turn (if retains it can't attack again) ✔️[3][11]
Example Question:
After applying the effect of "Dragocytos Corrupted Nethersoul Dragon" which allows it to attack a second time, once in the Battle Step again, "Book of Moon" is activated a changes "Dragocytos Corrupted Nethersoul Dragon" to face-down Defense Position. Will it still be able to attack a second time after this?
Answer:
After applying this effect, if "Dragocytos Corrupted Nethersoul Dragon" becomes face-down, the effect no longer applies. (During the same Battle Step, even if "Dragocytos Corrupted Nethersoul Dragon" returns to face-up Attack Position, it cannot attack again.)

If that monster was Set, Normal, Tribute, or Special Summoned, including the type of Special Summon (Ritual, Fusion, Synchro, Xyz, Link)

Exceptions below 		✔️[5][6][7][21]
Example Question:
If a Fusion Summoned "Vision HERO Trinity" is flipped face-down, or banished by "Interdimensional Matter Transporter", will its ATK still be doubled when it returns to being face-up?
Additionally, will its effect that allows it to make a second and third attack during each Battle Phase still be applying?
Answer:
If "Vision HERO Trinity" is flipped face-down, or temporarily banished by "Interdimensional Matter Transporter", its ATK will return to the 2500 printed on the card.
Additionally, the effect that allows it to make a second and third attack during each Battle Phase will still be applied.
✔️[7][10][12][17]
Example Question:
I control a "Red Wyvern", whose effect, which "can only be used once while this card is face-up on the field," has already been used. "Red Wyvern" is now banished until the End Phase by "Interdimensional Matter Transporter".
Once it returns, can I use "Red Wyvern"'s effect?
Answer:
Yes, you will be able to.
This also applies to the other monster effects, among others: "Baronne de Fleur"'s second effect and "Ogdoabyss, the Ogdoadic Overlord"'s second effect (though note they cannot be used twice during the same turn); also "Silent Paladin"'s second effect, "Schuberta the Melodious Maestra"'s first effect, "One-Eyed Skill Gainer"'s effect, "Suijin"'s effect, "Kazejin"'s effect, and "Sanga of the Thunder"'s effect.
If a Gemini monster was Normal Summoned again ❌ ❌[8]
Example Question:
"Elemental HERO Neos Alius" is Special Summoned by the effect of "A Hero Lives", and then Normal Summoned again so that its effect that treats its name as "Elemental HERO Neos" is applying. Afterward, "Elemental HERO Neos Alius" is banished by the effect of "Interdimensional Matter Transporter", and then returned to the field during the End Phase. In this case, is it possible to equip "Elemental HERO Neos Alius" with "Bashing Shield"?
Answer:
In this scenario, after "Elemental HERO Neos Alius" is banished by the effect of "Interdimensional Matter Transporter" and subsequently returned to the field, it is treated as a Normal Monster again and its effect that treats its name as "Elemental HERO Neos" is no longer applied. Even in this case, "Elemental HERO Neos Alius" is still treated as a Normal Summoned monster, and so it can be equipped with "Bashing Shield".
If that monster was Pendulum Summoned ❌[14]
Example Question:
An "Odd-Eyes Phantom Dragon" that was Special Summoned by a Pendulum Summon was changed to face-down Defense Position by the effect of "Book of Moon". Later, that "Odd-Eyes Phantom Dragon" was Flip Summoned, and inflicted battle damage to the opponent while attacking during the Battle Phase. In this case, can its monster effect of being a Pendulum Summoned card that inflicted battle damage to your opponent be activated?
Answer:
If an "Odd-Eyes Phantom Dragon" that was Pendulum Summoned is changed to face-down Defense Position, even if it's later returned to being face-up, that monster is no longer treated as being Special Summoned by a Pendulum Summon. Therefore, the monster effect of being a Pendulum Summoned card that inflicted battle damage to your opponent cannot be activated.
✔️[13]
Example Question:
An "Odd-Eyes Phantom Dragon" that was Special Summoned by a Pendulum Summon was banished until the End Phase by the effect of "Interdimensional Matter Transporter". Later, if the "Odd-Eyes Phantom Dragon" that returned to the field attacks and inflicts battle damage to the opponent, can its monster effect of being a Pendulum Summoned card that inflicted battle damage to your opponent be activated?
Answer:
If an "Odd-Eyes Phantom Dragon" that was Pendulum Summoned was banished by the effect of "Interdimensional Matter Transporter", that "Odd-Eyes Phantom Dragon" that returned to the field is treated as being Special Summoned by a Pendulum Summon, just like before it was banished. Therefore, the monster effect of being a Pendulum Summoned card that inflicted battle damage to your opponent can be activated.
If that monster was Flip Summoned ❌ ❌
The turn that monster was Set, Normal, Tribute, or Special Summoned ❌[24]
Example Question:
My "Raidraptor - Vanishing Lanius", which was Normal or Special Summoned this turn, is flipped face-down, then face-up again. Can I now activate "Raidraptor - Vanishing Lanius"'s "if this card was Normal or Special Summoned this turn" effect?
Answer:
No, you cannot.
In order to activate "Raidraptor - Vanishing Lanius"' "if this card was Normal or Special Summoned this turn" effect, it must remain face-up in the Monster Zone from the time it is Normal or Special Summoned, to the time you activate its effect.
❌[18]
Example Question:
After I Special Summon "Sky Striker Ace - Shizuku", during the same turn, it is banished temporarily by "S:P Little Knight"'s effect. After "Sky Striker Ace - Shizuku" returns to the field during the End Phase, can I activate its "during the End Phase, if this card was Special Summoned this turn" effect?
Answer:
No, you cannot.
In order to activate "Sky Striker Ace - Shizuku"'s "during the End Phase, if this card was Special Summoned this turn" effect, it must remain faceup in the Monster Zone from the time it is Special Summoned until the End Phase.
