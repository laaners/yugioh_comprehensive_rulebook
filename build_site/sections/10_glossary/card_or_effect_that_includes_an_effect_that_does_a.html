Card or effect that includes an effect that does [A]

Examples
Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring

When a card or effect is activated that includes any of these effects (Quick Effect): You can discard this card; negate that effect.
● Add a card from the Deck to the hand.
● Special Summon from the Deck.
● Send a card from the Deck to the GY.
You can only use this effect of "Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring" once per turn.

Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion

When a card or effect is activated that includes any of these effects (Quick Effect): You can discard this card; negate that activation.
● Add a card(s) from the GY to the hand, Deck, and/or Extra Deck.
● Special Summon a Monster Card(s) from the GY.
● Banish a card(s) from the GY.
You can only use this effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion" once per turn.

Dominus Impulse

If your opponent controls a card, you can activate this card from your hand. When a card or effect is activated that includes an effect that Special Summons a monster(s): Negate that effect, then if you have a Trap in your GY, destroy that card. If you activated this card from your hand, you cannot activate the effects of LIGHT, EARTH, and WIND monsters for the rest of this Duel. You can only activate 1 "Dominus Impulse" per turn.
  • A card or an effect that "includes an effect that does [A]" is still treated as such even if action [A] cannot be attempted due to another card effect[1].
    Example Question:
    Can "Solemn Warning" be chained to "The Flute of Summoning Kuriboh"? Also, if "Vanity's Emptiness" is applying, so that neither player can Special Summon, can "Solemn Warning" still be chained to "The Flute of Summoning Kuriboh"?
    Answer:
    In both cases, "Solemn Warning" can be chained.
  • You possess a fast effect (1) that can be chained when "a card or effect is activated that includes an effect that does [A]". If [A] does not or could not happen at resolution, at card or effect activation of (2) it must be determined if it is an effect that includes [A] in order to chain (1) to (2)[5]. Examples:
    • Question:
      When my opponent activates the effect of "Infernoid Devyaty" that negates the activation of a monster effect and banishes that monster, can I chain the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion" from my hand?
      Answer:
      In this scenario, if the effect of "Infernoid Devyaty" was chained to a monster effect that activated in the Graveyard, it is an effect that includes banishing a card from the Graveyard, so you can chain the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion". If the effect of "Infernoid Devyaty" was chained to a monster effect that activated outside of the Graveyard, such as the field or hand, you cannot chain the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion"[5].
    • Question:
      When my opponent activates the effect of "Fire Fighting Daruma Doll" that adds a Beast, Beast-Warrior, or Winged Beast-Type monster to their hand, can I chain the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion" from my hand?
      Answer:
      In this scenario, if the effect of your opponent's "Fire Fighting Daruma Doll" targeted a monster in the Graveyard, you can chain the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion". If the effect of "Fire Fighting Daruma Doll" targeted a banished monster, you cannot chain the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion"[6].
    • Question:
      The monster effect of "Toadally Awesome" is activated, which negates the activation of the card or effect it is responding to, destroys that card, and optionally Sets it to your field. Is it possible to activate "Solemn Warning", or the monster effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion", in response to this activation of "Toadally Awesome"'s effect?
      Answer:
      "Solemn Warning", or the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion", can only be activated in response to the activation of "Toadally Awesome"'s effect if that effect is responding to a Monster Card's monster effect, or the activation of a Pendulum Monster Card to the Pendulum Zone.
      Whether or not "Solemn Warning", or the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion", can be activated does not change depending on where, or how, the monster effect in question was activated[7].
    • Question:
      Can I activate the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion" in response to the first effect of "Drytron Alpha Thuban"?
      Answer:
      When the effect of "Drytron Alpha Thuban" is activated in the hand, it is an effect that Special Summons a monster from your hand, and not an effect that Special Summons a monster from the Graveyard. Therefore, you cannot activate the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion" in response to the effect of "Drytron Alpha Thuban" activated in your hand.
      When the effect of "Drytron Alpha Thuban" is activated in the Graveyard, it is an effect that Special Summons a monster from the Graveyard, and not an effect that Special Summons a monster from your hand. Therefore, you can activate the effect of "Ghost Belle & Haunted Mansion" in response to the effect of "Drytron Alpha Thuban" activated in the Graveyard[8].
  • You possess a fast effect (1) that can be chained when "a card or effect is activated that includes an effect that does [A]". If a card or effect (2) would do action [A] only under a certain [condition], if [condition] is checked at (2)'s activation[2]:
    • If [condition] is satisfied at the moment of (2)'s activation, therefore [A] could happen at resolution of (2), (1) can be chained.
    • If [condition] is not satisfied at the moment of (2)'s activation, therefore [A] cannot happen at resolution of (2), (1) cannot be chained.
    Examples:
    • Question:
      My opponent Link Summons "Knightmare Phoenix", and activates its "[...], then, if this card was co-linked when this effect was activated, you can draw 1 card" effect. Can I activate "Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring"'s effect, or "Dominus Purge", in response?
      Answer:
      If your opponent activates the effect of a co-linked "Knightmare Phoenix", you can activate "Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring"'s effect, or "Dominus Purge", in response.
      If your opponent activates the effect of a "Knightmare Phoenix" that is not co-linked, you cannot activate "Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring"'s effect, and cannot activate "Dominus Purge", in response.
      This also applies to "Haggard Lizardose"'s "[...], then if you banished a monster that was originally Reptile, draw 1 card" effect, depending on whether the monster banished to activate the effect was originally Reptile.
      Additionally, this also applies to "Knightmare Cerberus"'s effect, "Knightmare Unicorn"'s effect, and "Knightmare Gryphon"'s effect[2].
  • You possess a fast effect (1) that can be chained when "a card or effect is activated that includes an effect that does [A]". If a card or effect (2) would do action [A] of (2)'s effect but only under a certain [condition], if [condition] is checked at (2)'s resolution, (1) can be chained regardless if [condition] is satisfied or not ([A] can or cannot happen)[3]. Examples:
    • Question:
      When my opponent activates the effect of "Cross-Sheep", can I chain the effect of "Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring" from my hand?
      Answer:
      When your opponent activates the effect of "Cross-Sheep", you can chain the effect of "Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring". (It does not matter whether or not "Cross-Sheep" is pointing to a Ritual Monster when its effect is activated.)[3]
    • Question:
      When my opponent activates "Dual Avatar Defeating Evil", can I chain the effect of "Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring" in my hand?
      Answer:
      When your opponent activates "Dual Avatar Defeating Evil", you can chain the effect of "Ash Blossom & Joyous Spring". (It does not matter whether or not your opponent targeted a "Dual Avatar" Fusion Monster when activating "Dual Avatar Defeating Evil".)[4]
