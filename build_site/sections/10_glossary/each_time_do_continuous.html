Each time [A] happens, do [B] - Continuous Effects that apply at chain link resolution

Examples
Red-Eyes Flare Metal Dragon

2 Level 7 monsters
Cannot be destroyed by card effects while it has material. While this card has material, each time your opponent activates a card or effect, inflict 500 damage to your opponent immediately after it resolves. Once per turn (Quick Effect): You can detach 1 material from this card, then target 1 "Red-Eyes" Normal Monster in your GY; Special Summon it.

Kyoutou Waterfront

Each time a card(s) is sent from the field to the GY, place 1 Kaiju Counter on this card for each sent card (max. 5). Once per turn, while 3 or more Kaiju Counters are on this card: You can add 1 "Kaiju" monster from your Deck to your hand. If this card would be destroyed by a card effect, you can remove 1 Kaiju Counter from this card instead.
Powersink Stone

Each time a monster effect is activated, place 1 Spellstone Counter on this card (max. 2). While this card has 2 Spellstone Counters, neither player can activate the effects of face-up monsters on the field, also the effects of face-up monsters on the field are negated. Once per turn, during the End Phase, if this card has any Spellstone Counters: Remove them.
  • These are continuous effects that apply [B] at chain link resolution everytime [A] happens[1]. Examples:
    • Your opponent activates a card or effect, but you chain "Xyz Reborn" and Special Summon "Red-Eyes Flare Metal Dragon" from your Graveyard. The effect of "Red-Eyes Flare Metal Dragon" applies each time your opponent activates a card or effect while "Red-Eyes Flare Metal Dragon" has Xyz Material, and inflicts damage after that card or effect resolves. Therefore, in this situation, if your opponent activates a card or effect while "Red-Eyes Flare Metal Dragon" with Xyz Material is not present on your side of the field, the effect to inflict damage does not apply[1].
    • If "Compulsory Evacuation Device" is activated targeting your face-up "Fire Princess", and you chain "Emergency Provisions" by sending any number of Spell/Trap Cards you control to the Graveyard, when the effect of "Emergency Provisions" resolves at Chain Link 2, the effect of "Fire Princess" will apply to inflict 500 damage to your opponent. Then, then effect of "Compulsory Evacuation Device" at Chain Link 1 will be applied normally[2].
  • These are effects that do not start a chain (no colon nor semicolon)[3].
