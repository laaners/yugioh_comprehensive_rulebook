If [X] would be destroyed, you can do [A] instead: Destruction Substitution

Examples
Hugin the Runick Wings

2 "Runick" monsters
If this card is Special Summoned from the Extra Deck: You can discard 1 card; add 1 "Runick" Field Spell from your Deck to your hand. If another card(s) you control would be destroyed by card effect, you can banish this card you control instead. If this card on the field is destroyed by battle or card effect: Return this card to the Extra Deck.

Fire King Sanctuary

When this card is activated: You can place 1 "Fire King Island" from your Deck face-up in your Field Zone. Once per turn, if a card in your Field Zone would be destroyed by card effect, you can destroy 1 FIRE monster in your hand or face-up field instead. Once per turn, if your opponent Special Summons a monster(s), you can: Immediately after this effect resolves, Xyz Summon 1 FIRE Xyz Monster using only "Fire King" monsters you control. You can only activate 1 "Fire King Sanctuary" per turn.
Dark World Punishment

When a monster(s) would be Normal or Special Summoned: You can negate the Summon, and if you do, destroy that monster(s), then discard 1 Fiend monster. If a "Dark World" monster(s) you control would be destroyed by battle or an opponent's card effect, you can banish this card from your GY instead. You can only use each effect of "Dark World Punishment" once per turn.
  • A card whose effect reads "if (1) you control would be destroyed by battle, you can do (2)" can be applied when performing damage calculation[2]:
    • If you choose to apply the effect, immediately after damage calculation do (2), which can be banishing a card, detach a material for effect...
    • If you choose not to apply the effect, you can now use any effects that can be activated after damage calculation.
    Example A face-up "Bujintei Kagutsuchi" on my field has "Zoodiac Whiptail" as Xyz Material.
    • If "Bujintei Kagutsuchi" would be destroyed by battle, you can choose whether or not you will apply its effect when performing damage calculation. If you choose to apply the effect, you detach 1 Xyz Material from "Bujintei Kagutsuchi" immediately after damage calculation. In this case, it will no longer have "Zoodiac Whiptail" attached as an Xyz Material, so you cannot activate its gained effect.
    • If you choose not to apply the effect of "Bujintei Kagutsuchi", you can activate the effect gained from "Zoodiac Whiptail" after "Bujintei Kagutsuchi" has been determined to be destroyed in damage calculation. (The destroyed "Bujintei Kagutsuchi" will be sent to the Graveyard normally at the end of the Damage Step).
  • For this kind of effects we identify the card (1) that applies the substitution effect for effect destruction, the card (2) that is being destroyed, and the card (3) that is substituting for (2)'s destruction (sometimes (1) and (3) are the same card). Regarding substitution effects when both (2) and (3) are being simultaneously destroyed:
    • Mandatory substitution effects (1) applies even if the substituted card itself (3) would also be destroyed. In which case, only (1) will be destroyed[1].
      Example If both "Spirit of the Six Samurai" (1)=(3) and the monster (2) it is equipped to would be destroyed at the same time by an effect such as "Final Destiny", effect of "Spirit of the Six Samurai" will be applied, "Spirit of the Six Samurai" will be destroyed instead, and the monster it was equipped to will remain on the field.
    • Optional substitution effects (1) can be applied when the substituted card itself (3) is not being destroyed at the point in time the substitution effect (1) applies. In which case, only (1) will be destroyed[3].
      Example Your opponent activates "Icarus Attack", targeting "Megalosmasher X" (2) and "Lost World" (1) on your field. Even if "Lost World" and a Normal Monster on the field would be destroyed at the same time, as in this scenario, you can apply the effect of "Lost World" that destroys Dinosaur-Type monsters in your hand or Deck (3) instead. In this scenario, "Lost World" itself is destroyed by the effect of "Icarus Attack", but "Megalosmasher X" is not destroyed, and you destroy 1 Dinosaur-Type monster in your hand or Deck instead.
    • Optional substitution effects (1) cannot be applied if the substituted card itself (3) would also be destroyed at the point in time the substitution effect (1) applies. In which case both (2) and (3) will be destroyed[5].
      Example Your opponent activates "Icarus Attack", targeting your face-up "Orcustrated Babel" (2) in the Field Zone and "Orcustrated Core" (1)=(3) in the Spell & Trap Zone. In this scenario, since "Orcustrated Core" itself would be destroyed by "Icarus Attack", you cannot apply its effect and send it to the Graveyard instead.
    • Optional substitution effects (1) can be applied if the substituted card itself (3) would also be destroyed at the point in time the substitution effect (1) applies but (3) cannot be destroyed due to another card's effect. In which case, only (3) will be destroyed[4].
      Example "Ib the World Chalice Priestess" (1)=(3) is in your Main Monster Zone, and "Ningirsu the World Chalice Warrior" (2) is in a Main Monster Zone adjacent to it. In this situation, if your opponent activates "Dark Hole", since "Ib the World Chalice Priestess" is also linked to "Ningirsu the World Chalice Warrior", its first effect prevents it from being destroyed by battle or card effects, so it will not be destroyed by "Dark Hole". As a result, since "Ningirsu the World Chalice Warrior" would be destroyed, you can apply the effect of "Ib the World Chalice Priestess" and send it to the Graveyard instead.
