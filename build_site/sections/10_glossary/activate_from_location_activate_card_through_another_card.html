Activate [X] from [location]: Activate a Card through another Card's effect

Examples
Ignis Heat, the True Dracowarrior

To Tribute Summon this card face-up, you can Tribute a Continuous Spell/Trap you control, instead of a monster. Once per turn, when your opponent activates a card or effect while you control this Tribute Summoned monster (Quick Effect): You can take 1 "True Draco" or "True King" Continuous Spell from your Deck, and either activate it or add it to your hand.
Dream Mirror of Joy

During the End Phase: You can banish this card; activate 1 "Dream Mirror of Terror" directly from your hand or Deck. You can only use this effect of "Dream Mirror of Joy" once per turn. "Dream Mirror" monsters you control cannot be targeted by your opponent's card effects, or their monsters' attacks, except your "Dream Mirror" monster(s) with the highest Level. This effect is only applied while you control a LIGHT "Dream Mirror" monster.
Metaverse

Take 1 Field Spell from your Deck, and either activate it or add it to your hand.
  • If you activate a Field Spell Card (1) using the effect of another card (2), the activation of (1) will also be performed while (2) is resolving. No Chain Link is formed as a result of the (1)'s activation. If you activate (1) this way, you cannot apply (1)'s "when this card is activated" effect[1].
    Example If you activate a Field Spell Card using the effect of "Metaverse", the activation of the Field Spell Card will also be performed while "Metaverse" is resolving. No Chain Link is formed as a result of the Field Spell Card's activation. If you activate a Field Spell this way, you cannot apply that Field Spell's "when this card is activated" effect.
  • To activate [X] through another card effect you must still meet [X]'s activation requirements[2].
    Example "Soul Binding Gate" can be activated with the effects of "Metaverse" and "Demise of the Land", but you must still meet its requirements of controlling no monsters and Set Spell/Trap you control being destroyed by card effect this turn. Therefore, if you control no monsters and a Set Spell/Trap you control was destroyed by card effect this turn, you can activate "Soul Binding Gate" with the effect of "Metaverse". Also, when the opponent Special Summons a monster(s) during a turn in which a Set Spell/Trap you control was destroyed by card effect, and you control no monsters, you can activate "Soul Binding Gate" with "Demise of the Land"'s effect.
  • Trigger effects that say "if [X] is activated: do [A]" won't activate if [X] is activated by another card effect[3].
    Example When your opponent activates the effect of "Dinomight Knight, the True Dracofighter", they activate a "True King's Return" in the Spell & Trap Zone that is in the same column as your "Magical Musketeer Caspar". At this time, the effect of "Magical Musketeer Caspar" that adds a "Magical Musket" card from your Deck to your hand cannot be activated.
