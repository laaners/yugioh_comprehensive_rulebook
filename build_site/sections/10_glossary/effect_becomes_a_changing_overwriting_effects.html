Effect becomes [A]: changing/overwriting effects

Examples
Phantom of Yubel

1 "Yubel" monster + 1 Fiend monster with 0 ATK/DEF
Must be Special Summoned (from your Extra Deck) by shuffling the above cards from your hand, field, and/or GY into the Deck/Extra Deck. Cannot be used as Fusion Material. Cannot be destroyed by battle, also you take no damage from battles involving this card. When your opponent activates a monster effect (Quick Effect): You can Tribute this card; the activated effect becomes "Your opponent destroys 1 "Yubel" monster in their hand, Deck, or field". You can only use this effect of "Phantom of Yubel" once per turn.

Abyss Playhouse - Fantastic Theater

You can reveal 1 "Abyss Actor" Pendulum Monster and 1 "Abyss Script" Spell in your hand; add 1 "Abyss Script" Spell from your Deck to your hand, with a different name than that revealed Spell. While you control a Pendulum Summoned "Abyss Actor" Pendulum Monster, any monster effect activated by your opponent becomes "Destroy 1 Set Spell/Trap your opponent controls". You can only use each effect of "Abyss Playhouse - Fantastic Theater" once per turn.
Generaider Boss Room

When your opponent activates a card or effect in response to the activation of your "Generaider" card or effect: You can discard 1 card; your opponent's activated effect becomes "each player draws 1 card". You can only use this effect of "Generaider Boss Room" once per turn.
