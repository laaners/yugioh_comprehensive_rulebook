Special Summon [X] in face-down Defense Position

Examples
Gusto Falco

When this face-up card on the field is sent to the Graveyard, except by battle: You can Special Summon 1 "Gusto" monster from your Deck in face-down Defense Position.
Overlay Regen

Target 1 face-up Xyz Monster on the field; attach this card to it as material.
Burst Rebirth

Pay 2000 Life Points, then target 1 monster in your Graveyard; Special Summon that target in face-down Defense Position.
