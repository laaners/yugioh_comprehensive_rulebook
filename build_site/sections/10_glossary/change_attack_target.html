Change attack target

Examples
Construction Train Signal Red

When an opponent's monster declares an attack: You can Special Summon this card from your hand, and if you do, change the attack target to this card and proceed to damage calculation. This card cannot be destroyed by that battle.
Eye of Illusion

If you control an Illusion or Spellcaster monster: Activate 1 of these effects;
● Your Illusion and Spellcaster monsters cannot be destroyed by battle this turn.
● During your opponent's turn: Target 1 face-up monster your opponent controls; take control of it until the End Phase.
● When an opponent's monster declares an attack: Target 1 face-up monster your opponent controls, except the attacking monster; change the attack target to it and perform damage calculation.

Attack Guidance Armor

When a monster declares an attack: Activate 1 of these effects;
● Destroy the attacking monster.
● Target 1 monster on either field, except the attacking monster; change the attack target to it and perform damage calculation.
You can only activate 1 "Attack Guidance Armor" per turn.

  • When an effect that changes attack target on attack declaration is applied, Damage Step is entered and damage calculation is performed while the effect is resolving[1]. This means that:
    • Effects of cards that have to activate before damage calculation cannot be activated.
    • Effects of cards that have to activate during damage calculation cannot be activated
    • Effects of cards that have to activate at the end of the Damage Step can be activated.
    Example Question:
    When the second effect of "Number 38: Hope Harbinger Dragon Titanic Galaxy" is applied, can the effect of "Honest" be activated before damage calculation? During damage calculation, can "Forbidden Scripture" be activated?
    Answer:
    The effect of "Number 38: Hope Harbinger Dragon Titanic Galaxy" makes it the attack target, then performs damage calculation, so while the effect is resolving, the Damage Step is entered and damage calculation are conducted. Therefore, the effect of "Honest" cannot be activated before damage calculation, and "Forbidden Scripture" cannot be activated during damage calculation. However, effects that activate after damage calculation or at the end of the Damage Step can be activated normally.
