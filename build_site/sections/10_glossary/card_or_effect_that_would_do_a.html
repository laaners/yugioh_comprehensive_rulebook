Card or effect that would do [A]

Examples
Stardust Dragon

1 Tuner + 1+ non-Tuner monsters
When a card or effect is activated that would destroy a card(s) on the field (Quick Effect): You can Tribute this card; negate the activation, and if you do, destroy it. During the End Phase, if this effect was activated this turn (and was not negated): You can Special Summon this card from your GY.

Curse Reflection Doll

When a monster effect is activated that would inflict damage to you: Your opponent takes that damage instead. When you take battle damage, while this card is in your GY: You can Set this card. You can only use this effect of "Curse Reflection Doll" once per turn.
Starlight Road

When a card or effect is activated that would destroy 2 or more cards you control: Negate the effect, and if you do, destroy that card, then you can Special Summon 1 "Stardust Dragon" from your Extra Deck.
  • You possess a fast effect (1) that can be chained when "a card or effect is activated that would do [A]". At card or effect activation of (2), it must be determined if [A] happens or not in order to chain (1) to (2)[1].
    Example Question:
    If my opponent activates "Ceasefire", will the effect of a "Destiny HERO - Decider" in my Graveyard that adds it to my hand and reduces the effect damage to 0 activate?
    Answer:
    If your opponent activates "Ceasefire", you must determine whether or not it would inflict damage to you. For example, if the only monsters on the field are in face-down Defense Position, and/or are face-up Normal Monsters, the effect of "Ceasefire" is not an effect that you know will inflict damage to you, so the effect of "Destiny HERO - Decider" in your Graveyard does not activate. Note that if any face-up Effect Monsters are present on the field, the effect of "Destiny HERO - Decider" will activate.
  • You possess a fast effect (1) that can be chained when "a card or effect is activated that would do [A]". If [A] is determined to happen at resolution of a card or effect activation (2), (1) cannot be chained to (2)[2]. Examples:
    • Question:
      If "The Paths of Destiny" is activated, will the effect of a "Destiny HERO - Decider" in my Graveyard that adds it to my hand and reduces the effect damage to 0 activate?
      Answer:
      Whether or not the effect of "The Paths of Destiny" will inflict damage to you is determined when resolving it. Therefore, since it is not a card effect that you know will inflict damage to you, the effect of "Destiny HERO - Decider" in your Graveyard does not activate[2].
    • Question:
      If I activate the effect of "Gallis the Star Beast", can I chain the effect of "Performage Stilts Launcher"?
      Answer:
      When the effect of "Gallis the Star Beast" resolves, if the card sent from the top of your Deck to the Graveyard is a monster, you inflict damage to your opponent based on that monster's Level. Because the effect of "Gallis the Star Beast" is not guaranteed to inflict damage to your opponent, you cannot chain the effect of "Performage Stilts Launcher" to it[3].
