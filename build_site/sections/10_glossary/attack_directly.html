Attack directly

Examples
Wattgiraffe

This card can attack directly. If this card inflicts battle damage to your opponent by a direct attack: Your opponent cannot activate cards or effects until the end of this turn.
Infected Mail

Once per turn: You can target 1 Level 4 or lower monster you control; it can attack your opponent directly this turn. Send it to the Graveyard at the end of the Battle Phase.
Doble Passe

When an opponent's monster declares an attack on a face-up Attack Position monster you control: Make your opponent's attack a direct attack instead and inflict damage to your opponent equal to the ATK of the attack target you control. Also, that monster you control can attack your opponent directly during your next turn.
  • A monster's continuous effect says "while/if [condition] this card can attack your opponent directly". If it declares a direct attack, but something happens in the Battle Step such that [condition] is no longer satisfied, that monster can no longer attack directly with its own effect and a replay occurs (You can select a monster as the new attack target, or cancel the attack)[1].
    Example Question:
    While I have 2 cards in my Pendulum Zones with the same Pendulum Scales, I attack directly with "Metrognome" using its effect. During the Battle Step, if my opponent activates "Pendulum Shift" and changes the Pendulum Scale of a card in my Pendulum Zone, what happens to the attack?
    Answer:
    In this scenario, if "Metrognome" declares a direct attack using its own effect, but the Pendulum Scales of the cards in your Pendulum Zone are changed, "Metrognome" can no longer attack directly with its own effect and a replay occurs. (You can select a monster as the new attack target, or cancel the attack.)
