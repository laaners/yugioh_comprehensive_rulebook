Cannot be destroyed once/next/first time per turn

Examples
Dark Resonator

The first time this card would be destroyed by battle each turn, it is not destroyed.
Bubble Barrier

Each "Performapal" and "Performage" monster you control cannot be destroyed by battle or card effect once per turn. You take no battle damage from attacks involving "Performapal" and "Performage" monsters you control.
Phantom Knights' Wing

Target 1 face-up monster on the field; it gains 500 ATK, also the first time that target would be destroyed by battle or card effect this turn, it is not destroyed. You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "The Phantom Knights" monster in your GY; Special Summon it, but banish it when it leaves the field. You can only use this effect of "Phantom Knights' Wing" once per turn.
  • Multiple "cannot be destroyed once per turn" effects all apply to the first destruction[1].
    Example Question:
    If I have 2 copies of "Bubble Barrier" on my side of the field, will their effects prevent each "Performage" and "Performapal" monster on my side of the field from being destroyed twice per turn?
    Answer:
    If you have 2 copies of "Bubble Barrier" on your side of the field, since each of their effects are applied at the same time when a "Performage" or "Performapal" monster would be destroyed, each one will still only not be destroyed once each turn. (The effect is the same as if you were only controlling 1 "Bubble Barrier".)
  • "Cannot be destroyed once per turn" effects are not consumed if the card cannot be destroyed in the first place[2].
    Example Question:
    "Bubble Barrier" is face-up on my side of the field, as well as a "Performapal Silver Claw" with "Half Unbreak" applied to it.
    If "Performapal Silver Claw" battles a monster with higher ATK in this situation, will the first effect of "Bubble Barrier" be applied?
    Answer:
    In this situation, "Performapal Silver Claw" is already cannot be destroyed by battle due to the effect of "Half Unbreak". Therefore, if "Performapal Silver Claw" battles a monster with higher ATK, it is not destroyed due to the effect of "Half Unbreak", and the effect of "Bubble Barrier" is not applied.
