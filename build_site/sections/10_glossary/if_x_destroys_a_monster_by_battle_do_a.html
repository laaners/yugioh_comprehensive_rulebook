If [X] destroys a monster by battle: do [A]

Examples
Mighty Warrior

1 Tuner + 1 or more non-Tuner monsters
If this card destroys an opponent's monster by battle: Inflict damage to your opponent equal to half the destroyed monster's original ATK.

Break! Draw!

Equip only to a Machine monster. If the equipped monster destroys an opponent's monster by battle and sends it to the GY: Draw 1 card. Destroy this card during your 3rd End Phase after activation.
Horn of the Phantom Beast

Target 1 Beast or Beast-Warrior monster you control; equip this card to that target. It gains 800 ATK. If the equipped monster destroys your opponent's monster by battle and sends it to the GY: Draw 1 card.
  • This kind of effects will activate at the End of the Damage Step[1].
