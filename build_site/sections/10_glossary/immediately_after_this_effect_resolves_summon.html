Immediately after this effect resolves ... Summon

Examples
I:P Masquerena

2 non-Link Monsters
During your opponent's Main Phase, you can (Quick Effect): Immediately after this effect resolves, Link Summon 1 Link Monster using materials you control, including this card. You can only use this effect of "I:P Masquerena" once per turn. A Link Monster that used this card as material cannot be destroyed by your opponent's card effects.

Arrivalrivals

During the Battle Phase: Immediately after this effect resolves, Normal Summon 1 monster. You can only activate 1 "Arrivalrivals" per turn.
Urgent Tuning

During the Battle Phase: Immediately after this effect resolves, Synchro Summon 1 Synchro Monster using monsters you control.
  • If an effect that Summon states "Immediately after this effect resolves", it instructs a player to perform a Synchro, Xyz, Pendulum, or Link Summon at the resolution of the Chain Link[1].
  • A monster summoned by such effects is not considered to have been "Special Summoned by an activated Monster/Spell/Trap effect"[1].
  • If this kind of effect first summon some monsters from a location(s) to the field then performs the Special Summon with the monsters summoned:
    • You have to use all the monsters summoned to perform the Special Summon[2].
      Example If you activate "Geargia Change" by targeting exatctly 3 "Geargiano" monsters in your Graveyard, you must use all three of the Special Summoned monsters as material for the Xyz Summon. It is not possible to activate "Geargia Change" by targeting 3 monsters, and then only use 2 of the 3 Special Summoned monsters as material for the Xyz Summon.
    • The timing of summoning the monsters and then performing the Special Summon immediately afters are two separate timings that don't happen at the same time ("then" conjunctive)[3].
      Example You activate "Advanced Heraldry Art" while "Soul Levy" is face-up in your opponent's Spell & Trap Zone, and the effect of "Soul Levy" is applied each time you Special Summon. The effect of "Advanced Heraldry Art" Special Summons the 2 targeted "Heraldic Beast" monsters from your Graveyard, then finishes resolving. After "Advanced Heraldry Art" resolves, the effect of "Soul Levy" is applied and sends 3 cards from the top of your Deck to the Graveyard. Then, when you perform the Xyz Summon using the 2 Special Summoned monsters as materials, the effect of "Soul Levy" is applied again and sends another 3 cards from the top of your Deck to the Graveyard. In this case, the first set of 3 cards sent to the Graveyard and the second set of 3 cards sent to the Graveyard are not treated as being sent at the same time.
