Properties Changing: Attribute changing

Examples
Elemental Mistress Doriado

This card can only be Ritual Summoned with the Ritual Spell Card, "Doriado's Blessing". The Attribute of this card is also treated as WIND, WATER, FIRE, and EARTH while it is face-up on the field.
Armored Xyz

Target 1 face-up monster you control and 1 Xyz Monster in your GY; equip that monster from your GY to that monster on the field as an Equip Spell with these effects.
● Change the equipped monster's ATK to this card's ATK.
● The equipped monster gains this card's Attribute.
● At the end of the Damage Step, if the equipped monster attacked: You can send this card to the GY; that attacking monster can make a second attack in a row.

DNA Transplant

Activate by declaring 1 monster Attribute. All face-up monsters become that Attribute.
  • Monsters with multiple attributes count for all their attributes when used as material[1].
    Example "Genex Ally Triforce" is Synchro Summoned using "Genex Ally Birdman" and "Elemental Mistress Doriado" as Synchro Materials. In this scenario, since the effect of "Elemental Mistress Doriado" also treats it as a WIND, WATER, FIRE, and EARTH monster, "Genex Ally Triforce" is treated as having been Synchro Summoned using LIGHT, WIND, WATER, FIRE, and EARTH non-Tuner Synchro Materials. Therefore, the "● EARTH:", "● FIRE:", and "● LIGHT:" effects can all be applied.
  • A Multi-Attribute monster that shares one of its attributes with another monster and the latter are not treated as "Different Attributes" monsters[2].
    Example "Elemental HERO Sunrise" requires 2 "HERO" monsters with different Attributes as its materials. If you activate "Miracle Fusion" and use a LIGHT-Attribute "Elemental HERO Electrum" in your Graveyard along with WIND-Attribute "Elemental HERO Avian", you can Fusion Summon "Elemental HERO Sunrise". However, if "Elemental HERO Electrum" is face-up in the Monster Zone and its effect that treats it as WIND, WATER, FIRE, and EARTH-Attributes is applying, that "Elemental HERO Electrum" cannot be used as a material with "Elemental HERO Avian" to Fusion Summon "Elemental HERO Sunrise".
