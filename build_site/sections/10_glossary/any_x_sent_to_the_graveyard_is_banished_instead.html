Any [X] sent to the Graveyard is banished instead

Examples
Dimension Shifter

If you have no cards in your GY (Quick Effect): You can send this card from your hand to the GY; until the end of the next turn, any card sent to the GY is banished instead.
Dimensional Fissure

Any monster sent to the GY is banished instead.
Macro Cosmos

When this card is activated: You can Special Summon 1 "Helios - The Primordial Sun" from your hand or Deck. While this card is face-up on the field, any card sent to the GY is banished instead.
  • Monsters can still be Tributed. In that case, the Tributed monsters are not sent to the Graveyard and are banished instead[1].
  • A card that performs a Fusion Summon can be activated and Fusion Summon. In that case, the monsters used as Materials are not sent to the Graveyard and are banished instead[2].
  • You can still Synchro Summon. In that case, the monsters used as Materials are not sent to the Graveyard and are banished instead[3].
  • It is possible to Link Summon even if the monsters that would be sent to the Graveyard would be banished. In that case, the monsters used as materials are not sent to the Graveyard and are banished instead[4].
