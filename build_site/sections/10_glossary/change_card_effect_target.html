Change card/effect target to another appropriate target

Examples
Cairngorgon, Antiluminescent Knight

2 Level 4 monsters
During either player's turn, when another card or effect is activated that targets exactly 1 card on the field (and no other cards): You can detach 1 Xyz Material from this card, then target another card on the field that would be an appropriate target for that card/effect; that card/effect now targets the new target.

Shift

When your opponent targets exactly 1 monster you control (and no other cards) for an attack, or with a Spell/Trap effect: Target another monster you control that would be an appropriate target; that attack/Spell/Trap now targets the new target.
Fairy's Hand Mirror

When your opponent activates a Spell Card that targets exactly 1 monster (and no other cards) on the field: Target another card that would be an appropriate target; that Spell now targets the new target.
  • Your opponent has activated a card targeting a card (1) you control. If you chain a card/effect that changes the target to another appropriate card, (1) is still considered to have been targeted[1]. Examples:
    • Question:
      My opponent has activated "Tribute to The Doomed", targeting my "Spirit Reaper".
      If I chain "Fairy's Hand Mirror" and change the target to another monster, will "Spirit Reaper" still be destroyed by its own effect?
      Answer:
      Even though "Fairy's Hand Mirror" changes the target of "Tribute to The Doomed" to another monster, "Spirit Reaper" is still considered to have been targeted and will be destroyed by its own effect[1].
    • Question:
      When my opponent activates "Book of Moon" targeting my "Performapal Odd-Eyes Unicorn", I activate the effect of my "Cairngorgon, Antiluminescent Knight" in response, changing the target to "Cairngorgon, Antiluminescent Knight" itself.
      Do I then activate the Pendulum Effect of "Odd-Eyes Persona Dragon" during the End Phase?
      Answer:
      The Pendulum Effect of "Odd-Eyes Persona Dragon" will be activated if a face-up "Odd Eyes" Pendulum Monster in your Monster Zone (and no other cards) was targeted by an opponent's effect this turn while "Odd-Eyes Persona Dragon" was already in the Pendulum Zone.
      In the given situation, your opponent's "Book of Moon" resolves by targeting your "Cairngorgon, Antiluminescent Knight", due to that "Cairngorgon, Antiluminescent Knight"'s own effect. However, since "Book of Moon" was originally activated by your opponent by targeting "Performapal Odd-Eyes Unicorn", the Pendulum Effect of "Odd-Eyes Persona Dragon" must be activated during the End Phase of this turn[2].
