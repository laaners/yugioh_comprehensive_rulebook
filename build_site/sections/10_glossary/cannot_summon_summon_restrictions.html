Cannot Summon: Summon restrictions

Examples
Mozarta the Melodious Maestra

Once per turn: You can Special Summon 1 LIGHT Fairy-Type monster from your hand. You cannot Special Summon other monsters the turn you activate this effect, except for LIGHT monsters.
Power Filter

Neither player can Special Summon monsters with 1000 or less ATK.
Dimensional Barrier

Declare 1 monster card type (Ritual, Fusion, Synchro, Xyz, or Pendulum); for the rest of this turn, neither player can Special Summon monsters of the declared type, also negate the effects of all monsters of that type while they are on the field. You can only activate 1 "Dimensional Barrier" per turn.

Property changing

  • A card (1) with the restriction of "you cannot Normal or Special Summon except [...] monsters the turn you activate this card" applies its restrictions for the entire duration of the turn. Normally, if you Summon a monster (2) which violates the restriction of (1), then (1) cannot be activated. Similarly, if you activate (1) first (2) cannot be Summoned[1]. But if:
    • A card (3) has an effect that applies to (2) such that when (2) is Summoned it does not violate the restriction of (1), then (1) can be activated after (2) has been Summoned[1].
    • A card (3) has an effect that applies to (2) such that when (2) is Summoned it does not violate the restriction of (1), but you activate (1) before (2) is Summoned, then (2) cannot be Summoned anymore[1].
    Example: "A DNA Transplant" that was activated by declaring the LIGHT Attribute is face-up on the field. In this situation, I activate "Monster Reborn" and Special Summon "Darklord Superbia" from my Graveyard, then activate its effect and Special Summon "Mozarta the Melodious Maestra" from the Graveyard:
    • Even if you Special Summoned a "Darklord Superbia" (whose original Attribute is DARK) this turn, if you did so while the effect of "A DNA Transplant" was applying declaring LIGHT, you can activate the effect of "Mozarta the Melodious Maestra".
    • However, if you first activated the effect of "Mozarta the Melodious Maestra", you cannot Special Summon monsters whose original Attributes are anything other than LIGHT, even if the effect of "A DNA Transplant" is applying.
  • An effect (1) is applying so that a group of monsters with a certain specific property X cannot be summoned, but monsters with another specific property Y can be summoned. A monster (2)'s has X property, so (2) can't be summoned due to (1). If (2) has an effect that says "summon this card as a monster with Y property", then (2) can be summoned through that effect[2].
    Example: my opponent controls "Evilswarm Ophion", which has materials attached and is applying its effect "Level 5 or higher monsters cannot be Special Summoned". "Cockadoodledoo" has effects that say "you can Special Summon this card (from your hand) as a Level 3 monster" or "you can Special Summon this card (from your hand) as a Level 4 monster", so can Special Summon "Cockadoodledoo" using either of its effects.
