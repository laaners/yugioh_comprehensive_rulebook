Gain Life Points

Examples
Predaplant Moray Nepenthes

This card gains 200 ATK for each Predator Counter on the field. When this card destroys an opponent's monster by battle: You can equip that monster to this card. Once per turn: You can target 1 Monster Card equipped to this card by this card's effect; destroy it, and if you do, gain LP equal to its original ATK.
Mystik Wok

Tribute 1 monster. Select either its ATK or DEF and gain that many Life Points.
Rainbow Life

Discard 1 card; until the end of this turn, each time you would take damage, gain that amount of LP instead.

0 LP gain and Activation Legality

A card's effect which gains LP can be activated even if it will gain 0 LP and will resolve successfully.
Example: "Predaplant Moray Nepenthes" is equipped with a "Performapal Life Swordsman" by its second effect. Even if that monster has 0 ATK, you can also activate the effect that destroys an equipped monster and increases your Life Points by its ATK. In this case, the equipped monster is destroyed normally, but you gain no Life Points.
