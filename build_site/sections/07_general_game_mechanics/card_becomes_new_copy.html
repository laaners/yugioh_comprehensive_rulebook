Cards being treated as a different/new copy when changing location

  • Physical cards going from one area of the game to another (such as the hand/field to the GY) can cause effects to be unable to identify them as the same card for their purpose.
    Example Question:
    "Dinowrestler Pankratops"' effect that destroys a targeted card was activated by Tributing itself.
    If I chain the effect of "Invoked Mechaba" in response, will that "Dinowrestler Pankratops" be banished?
    Answer:
    If you activate the effect of "Invoked Mechaba" in response to the effect of "Dinowrestler Pankratops", that effect's activation will be negated. However, whether "Dinowrestler Pankratops" will be banished or not depends on it remaining on the field until the effect of "Invoked Mechaba" resolves.
    If "Dinowrestler Pankratops" is Tributed itself as a cost to activate its effect, the effect's activation will be negated due to the effect of "Invoked Mechaba". However, since "Dinowrestler Pankratops" that activated the effect is no longer on the field, it will not be banished.
    If "Dinowrestler Pankratops" Tributed another "Dinowrestler" monster to activate its effect, and that "Dinowrestler Pankratops" is still on the field when the effect of "Invoked Mechaba" resolves, it will be banished.
