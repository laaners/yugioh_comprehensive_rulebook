Text that specifies Special/Alternate Victory Conditions

Examples
Exodia the Forbidden One

If you have "Right Leg of the Forbidden One", "Left Leg of the Forbidden One", "Right Arm of the Forbidden One" and "Left Arm of the Forbidden One" in addition to this card in your hand, you win the Duel.
Victory Dragon

This card cannot be Special Summoned. To Tribute Summon this card, you must Tribute 3 Dragon-Type monsters. If this card attacks your opponent directly and reduces their Life Points to 0, you win the Match.
Final Countdown

Pay 2000 Life Points. After 20 turns have passed after you activate this card (counting the turn you activate this card as the 1st turn), you win the Duel.

Text that directly affects the state of the game like "you win the Duel" or "you win the Match" is text that is not an effect[1].

  • Even if those conditions are met during the resolution of an effect, if those conditions are not met after all the parts of the effect finish resolving, a victory cannot be declared[1][2].
    Example Question:
    With "Graceful Charity", "Dark World Dealings", "Solar Recharge", "Allure of Darkness", "Common Charity", or a similar card, I draw "Left Leg of the Forbidden One".
    If this completes all five pieces of "Exodia the Forbidden One", can I win the Duel without completing the resolution of the effect? (By discarding cards from my hand, etc.)
    Answer:
    You can only show your opponent all five pieces of "Exodia the Forbidden One" in your hand and win the duel after you have both drawn cards with the effect of "Graceful Charity" and then discarded cards from your hand.
    Therefore, even if you have all five pieces of "Exodia the Forbidden One" in your hand after you have drawn cards using "Graceful Charity" - if, after that, "Left Leg of the Forbidden One" is discarded by the effect of "Graceful Charity", you will not have completed "Exodia the Forbidden One", and will not be able to win the duel this way.
  • If those conditions are met during the resolution of a chain, a victory can be declared after each resolution[1][3].
    Example If you meet the Victory Condition of "Exodia the Forbidden One" by activating the effect of "Jar of Greed" that says "Draw 1 card" in a chain to the activation of the opponent's "Mind Crush", the effect of "Mind Crush" is not applied afterwards and you win the Duel[1].
  • If two alternate victory conditions are met at the same time, then the duel ends in a Draw[4].
    Example Question:
    Who wins the duel, if both players have all 5 cards required ("Left Leg of the Forbidden One", etc.) including "Exodia the Forbidden One" in their hand at the same time due to the effect of "Morphing Jar"?
    Answer:
    In this situation, the duel will end in a Draw.
