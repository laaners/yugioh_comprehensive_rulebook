For costs that read "return a card(s) to the hand;", cards must be returned to the hand in order to activate the effects.

  • If a card will be banished when it leaves the field, it cannot be returned to the hand as a cost to activate the effect of a card (1) that requires to do so, therefore (1) cannot be activated[1].
    Example Question:
    Can I return "Plaguespreader Zombie" Special Summoned with its own effect to Special Summon "Blackwing - Zephyros the Elite" with its own effect?
    Answer:
    Since "Plaguespreader Zombie" was Special Summoned with its own effect, it will be banished when it leaves the field, so you cannot be returned to the hand as a cost to activate the effect of "Blackwing - Zephyros the Elite".
    If "Plaguespreader Zombie" that was Special Summoned by its own effect becomes an Equip Card due to the effect "Immortal Phoenix Gearfried", the part of the effect that makes "Plaguespreader Zombie" being banished when it leaves the field is no longer applied. In that situation, you can return "Plaguespreader Zombie" that is equipped to "Immortal Phoenix Gearfried" as a cost to activate the effect of "Blackwing - Zephyros the Elite".
