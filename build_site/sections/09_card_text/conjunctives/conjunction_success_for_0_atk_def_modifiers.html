Conjunction success for 0 ATK/DEF modifiers

A Monster's (1) ATK/DEF is already 0. A card's (2) effect which involves:

  • Reducing (1)'s ATK/DEF with action [A] and then does an action [B], [B] will apply even if (1)'s ATK is already 0 and [A] is required such as "if you do" conjunctive[1].
    Example Question:
    When an attack is declared involving my "Predaplant Chimerafflesia" and an opponent's monster with less than 1000 ATK, can I activate the effect of "Predaplant Chimerafflesia" that decreases the ATK of the opponent's battling monster by 1000 and increases its own by 1000?
    Answer:
    Even when an attack is declared involving your "Predaplant Chimerafflesia" and an opponent's monster with less than 1000 ATK, you can activate the effect of "Predaplant Chimerafflesia". (You can even activate the effect if the opponent's monster has 0 ATK.) In that case, the effect will decrease the opponent's monster's ATK to 0, and "Predaplant Chimerafflesia" gains 1000 ATK normally.
  • Halving (1)'s ATK/DEF with action [A] and then does an action [B], [B] will apply even if (1)'s ATK is already 0 and [A] is required such as "if you do" conjunctive[2].
    Example Question:
    Can "TGX1-HL" be activated by targeting a "T.G." monster whose ATK and DEF are zero?
    Answer:
    You can activate "TGX1-HL" by targeting a "T.G." monster whose current ATK and DEF are both zero. If you do, the effect that destroys a Spell/Trap on the field is applied as usual.
  • Switching (1)'s ATK/DEF (when both are 0) with action [A] and then does an action [B], [B] will apply even if (1)'s ATK is already 0 and [A] is required such as "if you do" conjunctive[3].
    Example Question:
    Can a monster with 0 ATK and DEF be targeted to activated the Monster Effect of "Performage Mirror Conductor"?
    Answer:
    Yes, it can.
    Even in that case, the parts of the effect that swap the ATK and DEF and that inflict 500 damage resolves normally.
